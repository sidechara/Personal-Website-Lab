Writing

Welcome! I like to write and read books in my spare time. Some things I'm interested in include:

SFF (Science fiction and fantasy). In particular, I like "weird" fiction, i.e. stories that bend the genre expectations of SFF in meaningful ways.

Poetry. Poetry is a unique way of communicating about feelings and ideas, and I sometimes write poetry to enhance my own poetic sensitivities, or just to express myself.

Literary fiction. I love writing that makes me think, and I often seek to incorporate the qualities of literary prose in my own writing.

Critical essays. Finally, I also sometimes like reading essays about books to deepen my knowledge of textual analysis and the craft of writing.
